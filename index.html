About

10 years ago Roger Realty decided that buying a property was harder than it needed to be. He asked his father for a small loan of a million dollars so that he can purchase some real estate properties. He then fixed some up to be sold for a profit and some others to be rented. However, he found that dealing with conventional realtors was too difficult, went back to harvard to study real estate and gained his real estate license. 10 years later this is what he has created.

A Trusted Advisor

During the initial consultation, we get to know our clients and understand their ambitions and desires when looking for a new home. This allows us to offer a level of personalised service you wont find at other agencies. We have our clients best interest in mind at all times

Award-Winning Talent

Exceptional minds. That sums up our team at Realty. We are experts in Real Estate with our management team having close to 130 years of experience in the field. We offer cost effective solutions to your property needs.
